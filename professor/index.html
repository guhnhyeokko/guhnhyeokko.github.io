Research Interests

  • Underwater Acoustics for Bioacoustics
    • Bioacoustics research on marine mammals, specifically Indo-Pacific Bottlenose Dolphins in Jeju coastal waters.
    • Application of advanced acoustic analysis and machine learning to decode communication signals and behavior.
  • Measuring of Ambient Noise in Coastal Jeju
    • Investigation of various sources of ambient noise in coastal seas and their impact on marine life.
    • Study of underwater sound scattering phenomena for oceanographic monitoring and environmental assessment.
  • Biomedical Ultrasound for Understanding Hemodynamics
    • Non-invasive ultrasound imaging for assessing vascular structures and blood flow dynamics in large arteries.
    • Analysis of red blood cell backscattered signals to understand vascular physiology and diagnose cardiovascular conditions.
  • Therapeutic Ultrasound in Brain Applications
    • Development of novel ultrasound-based treatments for neurological conditions, specifically Alzheimer's disease.
    • Research on using ultrasound to enhance natural defenses and promote neuroregeneration in the brain.
